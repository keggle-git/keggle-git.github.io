I am a chemical engineer working towards a just energy transition. As an M.S. student at Carnegie Mellon University my research focuses on developing computational tools that can empower communities to use waste to reduce environmental pollution, create local jobs, and lower the cost of living.

Before graduate school, I founded the Non-Aligned Climate Action Project and ran my own consulting practice for over a year. Prior to that, I worked in industry for over 8 years at the Australian electrolyzer startup Hysata and in engineering consulting at Arup and Burns & McDonnell. I am a licensed Professional Engineer (PE) in the state of New York.

My research interests include causality, world models, waste valorization, and process systems engineering/optimization.

Past Projects

I have worked on over 35 industry projects with clients including the World Bank, US DFC, MTA, Ford, Dow-Dupont, Motiva, LyondellBasell, Valero, P66, Oneok, Citgo, SoCalGas, Rolls Royce, Delta Airlines, NYCEDC, and the US Embassy. A couple of my favorite projects include:
  • Hysata — BOP & Systems Engineer and Technoeconomic Modeling Lead. Developed load-following system models, built BOP simulation in Aspen HYSYS, and created integrated technoeconomic model for evaluating cost reduction potential.
  • UKPACT eThekwini Hydrogen Economy — Lead analyst for regional hydrogen strategy in Durban, South Africa. Infrastructure assessment, technoeconomic modeling, and hydrogen demand forecasting through 2050.
  • World Bank Chilean Hydrogen Study — Analyst and deputy project manager. Defined pathways for domestic hydrogen market deployment, regulatory review, and technoeconomic modeling of isolated grids.
  • Australian Government Water for Hydrogen — Chemical engineer for study on water supply considerations for hydrogen production. Authored sections of the publicly available technical paper.
  • Delta LaGuardia Airport — Lead mechanical engineer for $4B terminal reconfiguration. Front end planning through detailed design of mechanical scope including piping, HVAC, and fueling systems.
